Travel Tips

Why to Travel Light?
Many airlines charge extra for checked baggage. Consider packing only carry-on luggage to save on baggage fees.

Why to Opt for Budget Airlines?
Explore budget airlines, as they often offer lower fares compared to major carriers. Keep in mind that budget airlines might have additional fees.

How to book cheap flight tickets?
Booking your flight tickets well in advance (usually several weeks or even months) can help you secure lower prices. In some cases, booking a round-trip ticket may be cheaper than two one-way tickets.
